About Me

I am Amirhossein Zafarfard, born on February 24, 2005 in Mashhad, Iran. I am a professional football forward, currently playing with jersey number 11 for Istanbul Bozkurtspor in Turkey’s Amateur League 4. I have played in multiple youth teams in Iran and was selected for the Khorasan Razavi provincial team in 2023–2024.

I have been the top scorer of Mashhad and provincial leagues four times. My height is 184 cm and my weight is 68 kg. I migrated to Turkey alone to pursue my football career. I’m known for my powerful mindset and dedication. I walk over 8 kilometers daily and train relentlessly to improve my game.
