About Me

I’m Amirhossein Zafarfard, born on February 24, 2005 in Mashhad, Iran. I’m a forward with a height of 184cm and a weight of 68kg. Currently playing for Istanbul Bozkurtspor in Turkey’s Amateur League (4th tier) wearing jersey number 11.

I have been the top scorer four times in both Mashhad and Khorasan provincial leagues. I have represented the Khorasan province elite squad (2023–2024).

My dream is to reach La Liga and play for Real Madrid one day. I also aspire to compete in the Portuguese and Spanish leagues, showcasing my talents on the biggest stages in Europe.
