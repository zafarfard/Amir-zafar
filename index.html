About Me

I’m Amirhossein Zafarfard, born February 24, 2005, in Mashhad, Iran. I’m a forward footballer currently playing for Istanbul Bozkurtspor, wearing number 11. Standing at 184 cm and weighing 68 kg, I’ve played for various youth teams in Iran and was the top scorer four times in Mashhad and Khorasan leagues. My Instagram: @zafarfard11.

Career History

Goals & Aspirations
